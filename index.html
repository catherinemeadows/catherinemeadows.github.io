About

I am a Senior at George Washington University, majoring in Computer Science (BS) as well as minoring in Naval Science. I work as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant for the Computer Architecture course and as a Research Assistant at GW. My interests include networking and cybersecurity.

US Navy

I am a 1/C Midshipman in the United States Reserve Officer Training Corps (ROTC), Capital Battalion.
